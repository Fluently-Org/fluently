Contribute a New Framework

📄

1. Write a YAML file

Create frameworks/your-framework.yaml with id, name, dimensions, and description.

✅

2. Validate & index

CI runs validate-frameworks.js and regenerates frameworks/index.json automatically on merge.

🔗

3. Add knowledge cycles

Knowledge YAML files tagged with your framework_id will be validated against your framework's dimension keys.
